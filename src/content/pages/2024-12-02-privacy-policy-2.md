This Privacy Policy describes how illphated.com ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our Android applications (our "Services").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By downloading, installing, or using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

 

#### **1. Information We Collect**

 

We may collect different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Services to you.

**a) Information You Provide to Us**

We collect information you provide directly to us. For example:

* When you create an account, we may collect your username, email address, and password.
* When you contact us for customer support, we may collect your name, email address, and the contents of your message.

**b) Information We Collect Automatically**

When you use our Services, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including:

* **Device Information:** We may collect your device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID), and mobile network information.
* **Usage Data:** We collect information about your interaction with our Services, such as the features you use, the pages you view, the time and date of your visits, time spent on those pages, and other diagnostic data.
* **Crash Reports and Log Data:** We may collect diagnostic data in the case of an app crash or error. This information may include your device's IP address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when the error occurred, the time and date, and other statistics.

**c) Information from Third Parties**

We may use third-party services that collect information. These third parties have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

 

#### **2. How We Use Your Information**

 

We use the collected data for various purposes:

* **To Provide and Maintain our Services:** To deliver the core functionality of our apps.
* **To Improve and Personalize our Services:** To understand how users interact with our apps so we can enhance the user experience and develop new features.
* **To Communicate with You:** To respond to your requests, provide customer support, and send you important notices or updates.
* **For Security and Fraud Prevention:** To monitor for and prevent suspicious or fraudulent activity.
* To display relevant advertisements within our apps. You may be able to opt-out of personalized advertising through your device's settings.
* **To Comply with Legal Obligations:** To enforce our terms and comply with legal requirements.
