🚀 How Many Toyota Corollas Could a B-2 Bomber Technically Carry?
It’s one of those bizarre questions that blends military might with everyday curiosity:
How many Toyota Corollas could a B-2 Spirit Stealth Bomber technically carry?

Let’s dive into the math—and we’ll focus purely on weight capacity, ignoring cargo bay dimensions or feasibility. This is all about raw lifting power.

✈️ B-2 Bomber Payload Capacity (By The Numbers)
The Northrop Grumman B-2 Spirit, one of the most advanced and expensive stealth bombers in the world, has an impressive maximum payload of:

40,000 pounds (18,143 kilograms)

This is the total weight it can carry, typically made up of bombs, missiles, and military hardware. But what if it were filled with Toyota Corollas instead?

🚗 Average Weight of a Toyota Corolla
The modern Toyota Corolla, a fuel-efficient and globally popular compact sedan, weighs approximately:

3,000 pounds (1,360 kilograms)

That’s the curb weight, which includes all necessary fluids and a full tank of gas—but not passengers or cargo.
