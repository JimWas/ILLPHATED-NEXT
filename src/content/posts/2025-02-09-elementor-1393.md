# USB 3.0 Front Side

## Type-A

### Receptacle (F)


1: Vbus (Red)

2: D- (White)

3: D+ (Green)

4: GND (Black)

5: SSRX-

6: SSRX+

7: GND_DRAIN

8: SSTX-

9: SSTX+

### Plug (M)


1: Vbus (Red)

2: D- (White)

3: D+ (Green)

4: GND (Black)

5: SSRX-

6: SSRX+

7: GND_DRAIN

8: SSTX-

9: SSTX+

## Type-B

### Receptacle (F)


1: Vbus

2: D-

3: D+

4: GND

5: SSRX-

6: SSRX+

7: GND_DRAIN

8: SSTX-

9: SSTX+

### Plug (M)


1: Vbus

2: D-

3: D+

4: GND

5: SSRX-

6: SSRX+

7: GND_DRAIN

8: SSTX-

9: SSTX+

Vbus: Power | D-/D+: Data | SSRX/SSTX: Superspeed RX/TX
