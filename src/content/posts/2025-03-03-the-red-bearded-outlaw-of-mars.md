The Red-Bearded Outlaw of Mars

The neon haze flickered against the crimson dust of Mars as the lone cowboy stood his ground. His red hair and beard glowed under the city’s vaporwave lights, a relic of the old world in a future that had forgotten it.

Behind him, holographic billboards pulsed with cryptic messages in alien dialects. Before him, a pack of wild creatures—part earthly, part unknown—circled in silent reverence. A cyber-wolf with gleaming metallic eyes, a tiger with bioluminescent stripes, and a deer whose antlers shimmered like star clusters. They weren’t just beasts; they were survivors, like him.

The bounty hunters had come and gone, their greed no match for the legend of the Red-Bearded Outlaw. He tipped his wide-brimmed hat, scanning the Martian skyline, the neon metropolis humming like a synthwave dream.

A new frontier. A new law. His law.
