You can reshape your cowboy hat at home using simple household items. Here’s an easy way to do it:

Steam Method (Best for Felt & Wool Hats)

	1.	Boil Water: Heat some water in a kettle or a pot until it produces a steady stream of steam.
	2.	Steam the Brim: Hold the hat over the steam, focusing on the areas that need reshaping. Be careful not to burn yourself.
	3.	Shape by Hand: Once the material softens, gently mold the brim with your hands to the desired curve.
	4.	Hold the Shape: Keep it in position until it starts to cool and stiffen.
	5.	Let It Dry: Set the hat on a clean, dry surface and let it air dry completely.

Towel & Cup Method (For Straw Hats)

	1.	Dampen the Brim: Lightly mist the brim with warm water.
	2.	Shape Using a Cup: Place a cup or bowl underneath the brim where you want the curve.
	3.	Wrap with a Towel: Drape a towel over it to hold the shape.
	4.	Let It Sit: Leave it for a few hours until dry.

If you want a firmer hold, you can use a little hairspray after reshaping. Let me know if you need more help!
