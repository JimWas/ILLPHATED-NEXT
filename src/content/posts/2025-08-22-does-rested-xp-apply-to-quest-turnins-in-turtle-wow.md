No — in Turtle WoW (same as in original Vanilla WoW mechanics), rested XP only applies to mob kill experience, not to quest turn-ins, exploration XP, or other sources.

So:

✅ Killing mobs → gets the 200% rested bonus.

❌ Quest XP → no rested bonus.

❌ Exploration XP → no rested bonus.

❌ PvP honor XP (like from battlegrounds) → no rested bonus.

If you want to maximize rested XP efficiency, the classic trick is to do quests but don’t turn them in until after your rested XP is burned up on mob grinding. That way you’re not “wasting” rested XP on quest turn-ins.
