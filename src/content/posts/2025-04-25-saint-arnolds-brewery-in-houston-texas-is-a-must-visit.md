Discover the Heart of Houston Craft Beer at Saint Arnold Brewing Company

If you’re a fan of great beer and Texas hospitality, then Saint Arnold Brewing Company should be at the top of your must-visit list in Houston. As Texas’ oldest craft brewery, Saint Arnold has been serving up award-winning brews and good vibes since 1994 — and it’s only gotten better with time.

Located just north of Downtown Houston, this brewery isn’t just a place to grab a cold pint — it’s a full-blown experience. From the bold character of their art-filled Beer Garden & Restaurant to the wide variety of house-made beers (and ciders), there’s something for every taste. Whether you’re into hoppy IPAs, smooth stouts, crisp lagers, or creative seasonal blends, you’ll find something that hits the spot.

Take a Tour, Sip Some Magic

Want to see where the magic happens? Saint Arnold offers guided brewery tours that dive into the history, process, and passion behind every batch they brew.

Tour Times:

	•	Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays: 3 PM, 5 PM, and 7 PM
	•	Weekdays (Monday–Thursday): 6 PM

Each tour lasts about 45 minutes and includes samples of Saint Arnold’s finest brews — so show up thirsty and curious!

More Than Just Beer

Beyond the brew, Saint Arnold hosts events, art markets, live music, and is home to one of the most visually striking beer gardens in Texas. The vibes are chill, the staff is friendly, and the food? Way more than your typical pub fare — think elevated eats made to pair perfectly with whatever you’re drinking.

Whether you’re a local or just passing through Houston, a visit to Saint Arnold Brewing Company is a toast to Texas pride, creativity, and damn good beer.

Cheers!
