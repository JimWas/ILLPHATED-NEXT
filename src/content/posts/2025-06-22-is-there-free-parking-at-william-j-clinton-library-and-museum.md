Yes, free parking is available at the William J. Clinton Library and Museum in Little Rock, Arkansas.

Here are the details:
	•	Location: Parking lot at 1200 President Clinton Avenue, directly in front of the museum.
	•	Cost: Free for all visitors.
	•	Duration: No enforced time limits during normal visiting hours.
	•	Overflow Options: If the main lot is full, there is usually additional free street parking nearby, especially on quieter weekdays.
