If you're playing a High Elf on Turtle WoW and want to enable War Mode, here's how you can do it:

Enabling War Mode as a High Elf

Glyph Master Location
The High Elf Glyph Master is located in the basement of the starting building in the High Elf starting area. That’s where you can obtain the War Mode Glyph. 
Turtle WoW

Using the Glyph to Activate War Mode

Talk to the Glyph Master and purchase the War Mode Glyph.

Using the glyph will flag your character for PvP, granting access to open-world PvP benefits such as bonus experience or honor, depending on the system setup. 
Turtle WoW
+1

Toggling War Mode Off/On

You can return to the Glyph Master to deactivate War Mode. 
Turtle WoW

Depending on server rules or recent updates, once you deactivate War Mode, you may be able to reactivate it—but in some cases, that ability could be limited or permanent. In recent changes, however, reactivation via Battlemaster is supported. 
Turtle WoW
+1

Alternative: Battlemaster in Capital (for Newer Updates)

As of early August 2025, an update to War Mode mechanics has introduced a different method of toggling on/off through Battlemaster NPCs in capital cities:

Enable/Disable War Mode: Speak to a Battlemaster in your capital city to toggle War Mode at any time. 
Turtle WoW

Important Changes in the August 2025 Patch:

War Mode is now a permanent PvP flagging system until level 60.

Offers a suite of bonuses such as:

XP for killing players (similar to killing two mobs).

+5% XP from standard mob kills in the open world.

+20% XP in Battlegrounds and Arenas, on top of normal XP sources. 
Turtle WoW

Summary Comparison
Method	Details
Glyph Master (High Elf Start Zone)	Found in basement of your starting building. Purchase/use glyph to toggle War Mode.
Battlemaster (Capital City)	Introduced in Aug 2025 update. Allows toggling War Mode at will, with new PvP‐based XP bonuses.
Which Should You Use?

If you're just starting out as a High Elf, head to the Glyph Master in your starting building—this is the traditional method to enable War Mode early on.

If you've progressed further or prefer the newer system with dynamic toggling and added PvP rewards, consider using a Battlemaster in your capital city once you're able to access the capital.

Final Thoughts

For a fresh High Elf character, your best bet is to head downstairs and find that Glyph Master to enable War Mode quickly in the early game. As your character advances or if you're starting a new one, the August 2025 patch makes using War Mode more flexible and rewarding via Battlemaster NPCs in capitals.
