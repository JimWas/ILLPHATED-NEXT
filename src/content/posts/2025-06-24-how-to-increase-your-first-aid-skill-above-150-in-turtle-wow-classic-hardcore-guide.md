How to Increase Your First Aid Skill Above 150 in Turtle WoW (Classic Hardcore Guide)
If you’re grinding your way through Turtle WoW and wondering how to raise your First Aid skill past 150, you’re not alone. Whether you’re a solo hardcore survivor or just trying to reduce downtime between pulls, First Aid is one of the most essential secondary professions in Classic WoW—and Turtle WoW is no exception.

In this quick and easy guide, we’ll break down exactly how to increase your First Aid skill above 150, where to go, what you need, and how to do it fast.

🩹 Why Level First Aid in Turtle WoW?
Heals without using mana

Cheap bandages using cloth from mobs

Great for hardcore or solo players

Required for Advanced First Aid bandages like Heavy Mageweave and Runecloth Bandages

🔐 Unlocking First Aid Above 150
To go beyond 150 in First Aid, you need to learn the “Expert First Aid” manual. Here’s how:

📍 Where to Learn Expert First Aid (Skill Cap 225)
Buy the “Expert First Aid – Under Wraps” manual:

Location: Theramore Isle (Alliance)
Vendor: Deneb Walker – inside the inn near the docks
(Coordinates: 66, 51)

Location: Brackenwall Village in Dustwallow Marsh (Horde)
Vendor: Balai Lok’Wein – inside the main hut
(Coordinates: 36, 30)

Cost: Around 1 gold – bring extra for bandage books:

“Manual: Heavy Silk Bandage”

“Manual: Mageweave Bandage”

Requirement: Must have First Aid skill at 125 to learn Expert.

📈 Power Leveling Tip
Once you hit 150 and buy the manuals, follow this cloth breakdown to reach 225 fast:

150–180: Heavy Silk Bandages

180–210: Mageweave Bandages

210–225: Heavy Mageweave Bandages

Pro Tip: Farm humanoids in Stranglethorn Vale, Tanaris, or the Hinterlands for tons of cloth drops.

🧠 What’s After 225?
To go beyond 225 (up to 300), you’ll need to:

Complete the Triage quest to become an Artisan in First Aid

🗺️ Quick Summary
Skill Cap	Requirement	Where to Learn
150 → 225	Expert First Aid Manual	Dustwallow Marsh (Alliance: Theramore, Horde: Brackenwall)

🔎 Final Tips
Stockpile Silk, Mageweave, and Runecloth early

Keep First Aid maxed as you level – especially for Hardcore

You can level this profession anywhere – no trainer visits required beyond the manuals
