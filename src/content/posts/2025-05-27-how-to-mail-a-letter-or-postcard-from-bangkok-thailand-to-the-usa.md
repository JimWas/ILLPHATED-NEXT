How to Mail a Letter or Postcard from Bangkok, Thailand to the USA

Planning to send a letter or postcard from Thailand to the United States? Whether you're sharing travel memories or mailing an important document, here’s a simple guide to mailing something from Bangkok to the USA.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Prepare Your Letter or Postcard

Use English for the U.S. address.

Example format:

Jane Doe  
123 Apple St  
Austin, TX 73301  
USA


2. Add Your Thai Return Address (Optional)

It’s smart to include your Thai address, either in the top left or on the back.

3. Visit a Thailand Post Office

Popular branches in Bangkok include:

MBK Center

CentralWorld

Terminal 21

Thailand Post HQ on Chaeng Watthana


Just tell the staff:

> “I want to send this to the USA.”



4. Buy the Correct Postage

Postcard: 15–20 THB

Letter (standard weight): 30–45 THB

The staff will weigh your item and apply stamps or give them to you.


5. Send It Off

Drop it in the red mailbox outside or hand it to the counter staff.


---

Tips for Travelers

Delivery time: 7–14 days (via airmail).

EMS option: For faster delivery and tracking.

Thailand Post App: Helpful for tracking and locating branches.
