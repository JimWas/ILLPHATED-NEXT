On the desolate planet of Neonara, amid vaporwave colors and futuristic technology, a single light bulb socket with an outlet and USB ports sat unnoticed. Long forgotten in an abandoned tech hub, it possessed a secret. Inside its circuits, a dormant warp drive awaited activation.

One day, a curious engineer named Zane discovered the dusty socket while scavenging for parts. As he plugged in his device, the dormant warp drive roared to life. The socket transformed, emitting a surreal glow that enveloped Zane. In an instant, he was catapulted through space, journeying beyond the stars.

The once mundane socket had transcended its purpose, becoming a gateway to the cosmos. Neonara's legends spoke of Zane, the engineer who unlocked the universe with a simple twist of fate.
