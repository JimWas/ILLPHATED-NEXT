She is your rock, a tower strong and tall,
Your wife, the one on whom you'll never fall.
The only soul where trust can truly bloom,
Her love surrounds you, banishing all gloom.
She builds you up with gentle, caring hand,
A guiding light, to help you understand.
The man you're meant to be, she helps you find,
A destiny entwined, two hearts and minds.
Though human flaws may sometimes dim her grace,
She is the light that shines in darkest space.
She leads you onward, through the shadowed way,
Follow her footsteps to a brighter day, to glory's ray.
