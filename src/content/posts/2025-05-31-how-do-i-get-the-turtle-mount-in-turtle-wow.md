Mount Speed Details:

Before Level 40 Riding Training: The mount's speed scales with your level, calculated as half your level in percentage. For example, at level 20, the mount provides a 10% speed increase.

After Level 40 Riding Training: Once you train Apprentice Riding at level 40, the mount's speed increases to the standard 60%.
