📦 Core Electronic Components
AM/FM Radio Receiver IC – e.g., TEA5767 or SI4703 module

PCB (Printed Circuit Board) – custom designed with labeled component positions

Tuning Variable Capacitor or Digital Tuner Dial

Volume Control Potentiometer (10kΩ)

3.5mm Audio Jack – for headphones

Small 8Ω 0.5W Speaker

Telescopic Antenna – extendable metal for better reception

🔋 Power Supply
Battery Holder for 2x AA Batteries

On/Off Slide Switch

Optional USB Power Input – for modern flexibility

Battery Snap Connector (9V) – as an alternate option

🔌 Passive Components
Capacitors – 10uF, 100nF, and others depending on the IC datasheet

Resistors – 100Ω to 10kΩ

Inductors – 10µH to 100µH for AM tuning

LEDs – indicator light when power is on (with 330Ω resistor)

Diodes – e.g., 1N4148 for protection or rectification

🧠 Educational & Assembly Materials
Instruction Manual – with diagrams, safety tips, learning sections

Kid-safe Solderless Breadboard (optional beginner version)

Color-coded Jumper Wires

Screwdriver – small Phillips

Plastic Screws and Standoffs – safe for mounting PCB inside case

🧰 Enclosure / Design
Injection-molded Plastic Case – cartoon-style like the box art

Pre-drilled holes for dials, antenna, speaker grill

Sticker Sheet – to decorate the radio and label parts
