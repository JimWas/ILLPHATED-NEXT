The Best Android Media Player Apps That Let You Loop Videos on RepeatWhether you're looping a relaxing nature video, replaying a favorite song, or running a video kiosk at an event, the ability to loop a video on Android can be essential. In this post, we’ll break down the best Android media player apps that allow you to repeat videos endlessly — perfect for autoplay, looping, or background display purposes.
✅ 1. VLC for Android – Best Free Video Loop PlayerVLC Media Player is one of the most popular and powerful free Android video players. Known for its wide format support and open-source nature, VLC makes it easy to loop any video or even a playlist.
🔄 Loop Feature: Yes — loop single video or playlist.
🎮 How to Use: Tap the screen while a video is playing → Tap the loop icon (two circular arrows).
📁 Formats Supported: MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, FLV, and more.
SEO Keywords: VLC loop video Android, VLC repeat mode Android, VLC video player for Android.
✅ 2. MX Player – Smooth Playback with Loop OptionsMX Player is another leading Android video player app offering a user-friendly interface and powerful playback controls. It’s widely used for high-performance video watching and looping capabilities.
🔁 Loop Feature: Yes — loop one or all videos.
🎮 How to Use: Tap three-dot menu → Tap Play → Choose Loop one or Loop all.
⚡ Extra: Hardware acceleration, subtitle sync, and gesture controls.
SEO Keywords: Loop video MX Player Android, MX Player repeat video, Android video player loop.
✅ 3. KMPlayer – Versatile with Cloud SupportKMPlayer is a modern, stylish Android video player with great loop support and added features like Google Drive playback.
🔁 Loop Feature: Yes — loop single video or playlist.
🎮 How to Use: Tap screen → Tap Repeat button until desired mode is active (One, All, Off).
☁️ Cloud Ready: Connect with Google Drive for seamless access.
SEO Keywords: KMPlayer loop video Android, KMPlayer repeat option, video playback loop Android.
✅ 4. GOM Player – Loop and Play 360° VideosGOM Player is ideal for those looking to loop videos and explore advanced media formats like 360-degree videos on Android.
🔁 Loop Feature: Yes.
🎮 How to Use: Tap screen → Hit the repeat icon in the player controls.
🌀 Bonus: 360° video playback support.
SEO Keywords: GOM Player Android repeat, GOM loop videos, Android loop playback player.
✅ 5. XPlayer – Clean UI with Powerful FeaturesXPlayer (also known as Video Player All Format) is a reliable media player app that supports all major formats and includes seamless looping.
🔁 Loop Feature: Yes.
🎮 How to Use: Tap screen → Tap Repeat icon to cycle through Repeat One, Repeat All, or Off.
📲 Clean Interface: Smooth, intuitive UI.
SEO Keywords: XPlayer loop video, repeat video Android player, Android repeat media app.
✅ 6. BSPlayer – Great for Pop-Up & Looping PlaybackBSPlayer supports pop-up video playback and custom playlist looping, making it excellent for looping videos while multitasking.
🔁 Loop Feature: Yes.
🎮 How to Use: Use the playlist screen or in-player settings to toggle loop modes.
🪟 Bonus: Floating pop-up video support.
SEO Keywords: BSPlayer Android loop, repeat video BSPlayer, Android video player loop mode.
✅ 7. Kodi – Playlist Loops for Power UsersKodi is a full media center application and a bit more advanced than others on this list. If you’re looking to create looping video playlists on Android, Kodi is for you.
🔁 Loop Feature: Yes (playlist loop).
🎮 How to Use: Create a playlist and enable loop or repeat in the playback settings.
💡 Power Tip: Great for digital signage or autoplay video kiosks.
SEO Keywords: Kodi loop playlist Android, Kodi repeat video, media center loop playback Android.
🔄 Final Thoughts: Which Android Video Player is Best for Looping?If you're simply looking to repeat a single video on Android, VLC and MX Player are your best bets — both are free, lightweight, and feature-packed. For more advanced needs like 360-degree looping or floating window playback, consider GOM Player or BSPlayer.
