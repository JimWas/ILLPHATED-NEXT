🏎️ Racing Circuits Near Bangkok

1. Bangkok Racing Circuit (BRC)
Located just north of Bangkok, BRC is an outdoor facility designed for various motorsport activities, including go-karting, motorcycle racing, and car racing. It's known for its accessibility and affordability, making it popular among local racers and casual enthusiasts.  

2. Thailand Circuit Motorsport Complex
Situated in Nakhon Chai Si, Nakhon Pathom Province, approximately an hour west of Bangkok, this complex is a national car racing venue covering over 20-30 rai (32,000-48,000 square meters). It hosts various motorsport events and offers facilities for both car and motorcycle racing.  

3. Pathum Thani Speedway
Located in Pathum Thani Province, just north of Bangkok, this speedway is popular among drift enthusiasts. It offers an open concrete pad suitable for drifting and other motorsport activities.  

4. E-Gokart by Monowheel
This indoor go-karting facility in Bangkok provides electric kart racing experiences suitable for all ages. It's a great place for beginners and those looking for a fun, safe racing environment.  


---

🐎 Horse Racing in Bangkok

Royal Bangkok Sports Club (RBSC)
Established in 1901, RBSC is an exclusive sports club in Bangkok known for its horse racing venue. Horse races are held every other Sunday and are open to the public, offering a glimpse into Thailand's equestrian traditions.  


---

🏁 Future Developments

Thailand is planning to bid for hosting a Formula One street race in Bangkok by 2028. The proposed "Sustainable F1" race aims to promote green technology alongside motorsport, potentially boosting tourism and the local economy.  


---

Whether you're interested in motorsport racing or horse racing, Bangkok and its vicinity offer a variety of venues to explore.
