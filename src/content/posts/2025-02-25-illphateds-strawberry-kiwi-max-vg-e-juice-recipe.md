Strawberry Kiwi Max VG E-Juice Recipe
A smooth, fruity blend with rich vapor production, designed for a high VG experience.

Ingredients (for 100mL batch, 20% flavor, 80VG/20PG ratio)
Vegetable Glycerin (VG): 80mL (80%)
Propylene Glycol (PG) (including flavoring): 20mL (20%)
Nicotine (Optional): Adjust based on preference (use PG-based nicotine for easier mixing)
Strawberry Flavoring: 12mL (12%)
Kiwi Flavoring: 8mL (8%)
Instructions:
Measure all ingredients using a precision scale or syringe for accuracy.
Combine the PG, flavorings, and nicotine (if using) in a clean mixing bottle.
Mix thoroughly by shaking well for at least 2 minutes.
Add VG slowly while stirring to prevent bubbles.
Shake well and let it steep for 3–7 days for best flavor development.
This mix delivers intense fruit flavor, thick clouds, and a smooth throat hit. Adjust the flavoring balance if you prefer more strawberry or kiwi dominance. 🍓🥝💨
