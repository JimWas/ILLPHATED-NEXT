💡 Tips for Using Ride-Hailing Apps in Bangkok
Payment Methods: While all three apps support cash payments, linking a card or digital wallet can expedite the process.

Peak Hours: Anticipate longer wait times and higher fares during rush hours (7-9 AM and 5-7 PM) and rainy seasons.

Safety First: Always verify the driver's details and vehicle plate before boarding.

Language Barriers: Utilize in-app chat features, which often include auto-translation, to communicate with drivers.

Airport Transfers: Both Suvarnabhumi and Don Mueang airports have designated ride-hailing pick-up zones.
