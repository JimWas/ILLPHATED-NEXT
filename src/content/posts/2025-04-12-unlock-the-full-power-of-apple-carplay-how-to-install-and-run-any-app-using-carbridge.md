Apple CarPlay is a great tool for staying connected while on the road—but let’s be honest, it’s also limiting. You're stuck with a small selection of apps, and Apple decides which ones make the cut. What if you want to use YouTube, Netflix, or Google Chrome on your CarPlay screen?

That's where CarBridge comes in.

In this blog post, I’ll walk you through how to install and run virtually any app on Apple CarPlay using CarBridge, a powerful jailbreak tweak that breaks Apple's walled garden wide open.

⚠️ First, a Quick Disclaimer
This guide involves jailbreaking your iPhone, which may void your warranty, reduce security, and cause instability. Proceed at your own risk.

🔧 What You'll Need
An iPhone running iOS 14 – 17 (jailbreak compatible)

A jailbroken iPhone (via tools like Taurine, Palera1n, or Dopamine, depending on iOS version)

The Sileo or Zebra package manager

CarBridge from Havoc

An Apple CarPlay-compatible vehicle

🚗 Step-by-Step Guide to Install CarBridge
Step 1: Jailbreak Your iPhone
Use a jailbreak tool compatible with your iOS version. You can find community-vetted tools at https://ios.cfw.guide.

📌 Note: If you're unsure what jailbreak is right for you, check your iOS version and use the guide above.

Step 2: Install Sileo or Zebra
Most jailbreak tools come bundled with Sileo or Zebra, which are app stores for jailbreak tweaks. If not, install one manually using your jailbreak tool’s terminal or loader app.

Step 3: Add the Havoc Repo
If you're using Sileo:

Open Sileo → Go to Sources → Tap +.

Add this URL: https://havoc.app

If you're using Zebra:

Go to Sources → Add Source → Enter the same URL.

Step 4: Search & Install CarBridge
Once the repo is added, search for CarBridge.

Tap Purchase (currently $9.99 at time of writing).

After payment, tap Install → Respring your device.

Step 5: Launch the CarBridge App
After respringing, you’ll find a new app called CarBridge on your home screen.

From there:

Open CarBridge

Tap App List

Choose the apps you want to mirror to Apple CarPlay

Customize layout, icons, and access permissions

🎥 What Apps Can You Use?
With CarBridge, you can run almost any app on your CarPlay screen—YouTube, Netflix, Safari, Maps alternatives, or even games. However, keep safety in mind—avoid watching videos or using distracting apps while driving.

✅ Tips & Best Practices
Use in Park: Only run entertainment or non-essential apps when the vehicle is stationary.

App Compatibility: Some apps may not render perfectly on the CarPlay UI. Trial and error is part of the fun.

Updates: Keep an eye out for iOS updates that may affect your jailbreak or CarBridge functionality.

Final Thoughts
CarBridge opens up Apple CarPlay’s true potential, giving you the freedom to run any app you want. Whether it’s for productivity, entertainment, or navigation, you’re in control. Just remember to stay safe and use responsibly.

Happy driving—and hacking! 🚗💻
