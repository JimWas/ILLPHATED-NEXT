An abstract expressionist portrait of a mysterious Southern cowgirl. She has striking green eyes and wavy blonde hair that partially flows from under a wide-brimmed black hat, adorned with a holy cross. A neck garter mask covers her neck but leaves her mouth and nose exposed. Her expression shows she's tired but motivated after a long day of work. In the background is a tattered, black and white American flag, missing stars. The painting style features thick, bold oil paint strokes and palette knife techniques, creating depth and texture. The color palette for the cowgirl is muted, dominated by shades of beige, cream, dusty blue, burnt orange, and black, while the flag is strictly black and white. The background has a rough, patchy texture, complementing the chaotic, expressive brushwork of the figure. The overall atmosphere is raw, moody, and artistic, blending realism with abstraction. The signature of illphated is on the bottom corner.
