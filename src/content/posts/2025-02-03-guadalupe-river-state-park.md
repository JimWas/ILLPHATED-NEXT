It depends on what you’re looking for! Here’s a breakdown to help you decide:

Turkey Sink
✅ Pros:

50-amp hookups (better if you have a high-power RV setup)
More sites available (48 vs. 37)
Likely a bit more spacious
🚫 Cons:

No lantern post (if that matters to you)
Cedar Sage
✅ Pros:

Lantern post (nice for nighttime lighting if you’re tent camping)
Feels a bit more secluded (fewer sites = potentially quieter)
🚫 Cons:

Only 30-amp hookups (less power if you're using an RV)
Verdict?
If you're RV camping, Turkey Sink is the better choice due to the 50-amp hookup.
If you're tent camping, Cedar Sage might be nicer since it has a lantern post and could be quieter with fewer sites.
Both have water, fire rings, tent pads, and restrooms nearby—so you can’t go wrong either way! Do you have a preference for scenery or seclusion?
