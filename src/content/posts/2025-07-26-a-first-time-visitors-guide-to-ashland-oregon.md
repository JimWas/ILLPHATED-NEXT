🌲 A First-Time Visitor’s Guide to Ashland, Oregon

Explore Shakespeare, Mountains, and Artisan Magic in Southern Oregon

Nestled at the base of the Siskiyou Mountains, Ashland, Oregon is a haven for creatives, hikers, foodies, and anyone craving a vibrant mix of small-town charm and world-class arts. Whether you're here for a weekend or a week, this guide will help you experience the best of Ashland.


---

🎭 The Heartbeat: Oregon Shakespeare Festival (OSF)

Ashland is best known for the Oregon Shakespeare Festival, which runs from spring through fall. With multiple theaters—including the stunning outdoor Allen Elizabethan Theatre—OSF brings Shakespeare and modern plays to life in an unforgettable setting. Even if you're not a Shakespeare buff, the energy and talent are worth experiencing.

> 🎟️ Pro Tip: Buy tickets in advance, and check out the Green Show—free outdoor performances before evening plays.




---

🛍️ Downtown Ashland: Eclectic, Artistic, and Walkable

Wander through Ashland’s charming downtown packed with:

Independent Bookstores (Bloomsbury Books is a local treasure)

Crystal and Gem Shops, art galleries, and vintage stores

Cafés and bakeries—grab a latte at Noble Coffee Roasters or treats from Mix Bakeshop

Lithia Park entrances, meandering right off the plaza



---

🌿 Lithia Park & the Outdoors

Designed by the same landscape architect who worked on San Francisco’s Golden Gate Park, Lithia Park offers:

100 acres of manicured gardens, duck ponds, and forest trails

A serene Japanese garden

Opportunities for yoga, meditation, or simply a peaceful stroll along Ashland Creek


For the more adventurous:

Hike Grizzly Peak for panoramic views

Mt. Ashland offers skiing in winter and wildflower hikes in summer



---

🧘‍♀️ Wellness, Spas & Lithia Water

Sample the Lithia mineral water at downtown fountains. While the taste is sulfuric, locals swear by its health benefits. Afterward, indulge in wellness:

Jackson Wellsprings: A rustic spa experience with soaking tubs, sauna, and sacred events

Chozu Bath & Tea Gardens: Upscale Japanese-style soaking and massage



---

🍷 Wine, Food & Local Flavor

Southern Oregon is an underrated wine region. Don’t miss:

Weisinger Family Winery and Belle Fiore Estate for tastings with a view

Peerless Restaurant, Amuse, or Larks Home Kitchen Cuisine for farm-to-table cuisine

The Ashland Food Co-op for organic groceries and deli-style eats



---

🎨 Art, Culture & Events

Beyond Shakespeare:

First Friday Art Walks light up downtown galleries

ScienceWorks Hands-On Museum is fun for kids and adults alike

Ashland Independent Film Festival (AIFF) showcases bold storytelling each spring



---

🛏️ Where to Stay

Ashland Springs Hotel – a historic boutique hotel downtown

Lithia Springs Resort – spa-style lodging with soaking tubs

Charming Airbnbs nestled in the hills or near the theater district



---

🌄 Sample 2-Day Itinerary

Day 1:

Morning: Coffee + breakfast downtown

Late Morning: Explore Lithia Park

Afternoon: Shop downtown + OSF Green Show

Evening: Dinner at Larks + catch a Shakespeare performance


Day 2:

Morning: Hike Grizzly Peak or spa morning at Chozu

Afternoon: Wine tasting + late lunch at a vineyard

Evening: Enjoy live music at Oberon’s Tavern or relax with local ice cream



---

📍 Quick Tips

When to Visit: Spring through early fall for theater, late fall for fall colors, winter for cozy vibes + skiing

Getting There: Rogue Valley International Airport in Medford (15 min away), or drive via I-5

Dress Code: Casual outdoorsy-chic, with layers!



---

✨ Why You’ll Fall in Love with Ashland

Ashland is more than Shakespeare—it’s a place where mountain energy meets intellectual spirit. It's where artisan chocolate shops, meditative parks, world-class theater, and forest hikes are just blocks apart.

If you love a town that feeds both your soul and your senses, Ashland is calling.
