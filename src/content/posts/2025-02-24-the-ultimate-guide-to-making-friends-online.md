Making friends online is all about finding the right communities and being authentic in your interactions. Here are some solid ways to connect with like-minded people:

1. Join Online Communities & Forums
Reddit – Find a subreddit for your interests (r/makingfriends, r/gaming, r/cyberpunk, etc.).
Discord Servers – Join servers around your favorite games, music, or hobbies.
Facebook Groups – Many niche communities exist here.
2. Engage in Social Media & Content Creation
Twitter/X & Instagram – Follow and interact with people who share your passions.
Twitch & YouTube – Since you stream as Illphated, engaging with other streamers and viewers can build friendships.
3. Play Multiplayer Games
Games like Destiny 2, Final Fantasy XIV, League of Legends, GTA Online, and more have strong communities.
Be active in voice chats or text channels.
4. Try Friend-Making Apps
Bumble BFF – For finding platonic friendships.
Friender – Matches you based on common interests.
5. Join Special Interest Websites
Meetup.com – Find virtual meetups in your area of interest.
Couchsurfing Hangouts – Even if you’re not traveling, it has a great chat feature for meeting new people.
6. Be Active in Conversations
Comment on people’s posts genuinely, reply in group chats, and ask engaging questions.
Don’t be afraid to DM someone if you vibe with their content.
7. Start Your Own Community
If you can’t find a space that fits, create a Discord server, subreddit, or even a website related to your interests!
