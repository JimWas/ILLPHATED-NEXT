In addition to granting you and your carload of guests unlimited entry to more than 80 state parks, your Parks Pass also gives you:

 

A subscription to the State Parks Getaways e-newsletter as well as park email updates to help you plan your next trip. To make sure you get our emails, add us to your address book by following the steps at the end of this email.

10% discount on eligible retail and rental items in many State Park Stores.

50% off your second night of camping, when you stay two or more consecutive nights in the same campsite or regular screen shelter

Discover park events with the Calendar of Events. Make your overnight or day use reservations online at any time, or call (512) 389-8900. Read more about being a state parks pass holder.
