Creating a mango milkshake e-liquid recipe requires balancing flavors to achieve a creamy, rich, and fruity profile. Here’s a well-rounded **Mango Milkshake Vape Juice Recipe** with **5% (50mg/mL) nicotine** in a **30mL** batch.

### **Ingredients (for 30mL total)**
- **Vegetable Glycerin (VG)** – 60% (18mL)  
- **Propylene Glycol (PG)** – 25% (7.5mL) *(including flavorings and nicotine solution)*  
- **Nicotine Base (100mg/mL in PG)** – 1.5mL (for 5% total strength)  
- **Mango Flavor Concentrate** – 6% (1.8mL) *(e.g., Capella Mango, TFA Mango, or FlavourArt Mango)*  
- **Vanilla Ice Cream Flavor** – 3% (0.9mL) *(TFA Vanilla Bean Ice Cream or similar)*  
- **Sweet Cream Flavor** – 2% (0.6mL) *(TFA Sweet Cream or FA Fresh Cream)*  
- **Graham Cracker or Cookie Flavor** – 1% (0.3mL) *(Optional for a milkshake base note)*  
- **Sweetener (Sucralose or Ethyl Maltol)** – 0.5% (0.15mL) *(Adjust to taste)*  

### **Instructions**
1. **Prepare Your Work Area** – Wear gloves, use a clean surface, and ensure proper ventilation.  
2. **Measure Nicotine** – Add **1.5mL** of **100mg/mL nicotine in PG** to a mixing container.  
3. **Add Flavorings** – Mix in the **Mango, Vanilla Ice Cream, Sweet Cream, Graham Cracker, and Sweetener** concentrates.  
4. **Add PG & VG** – Pour in the remaining PG first, then the VG.  
5. **Mix Well** – Shake the bottle vigorously for 2-3 minutes or use a magnetic stirrer.  
6. **Steeping** – Let it sit for at least **3-5 days** for the flavors to blend. A longer steep (1-2 weeks) will enhance creaminess.  
7. **Test & Adjust** – Taste-test and adjust sweetness or creaminess if needed.  

### **Final Profile**
- **Nicotine Strength**: **50mg/mL (5%)**  
- **VG/PG Ratio**: **60/40** *(Good balance for both flavor and vapor production)*  
- **Flavor Profile**: Creamy mango milkshake with a smooth, rich texture and a slight vanilla base.
