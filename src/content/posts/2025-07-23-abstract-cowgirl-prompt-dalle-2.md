Create a portrait of a mysterious figure (a southern cowgirl with stunning green eyes and beautiful blonde wavy hair) wearing a wide-brimmed black hat, with tousled blond hair partially covering their face. The style should be abstract and textured, resembling thick, bold oil paint strokes and palette knife techniques. Use a muted color palette dominated by shades of beige, cream, dusty blue, burnt orange, and black. The background should have a rough, patchy texture that complements the chaotic, expressive brushwork of the figure. The overall atmosphere should feel raw, moody, and artistic, blending realism with abstraction.
