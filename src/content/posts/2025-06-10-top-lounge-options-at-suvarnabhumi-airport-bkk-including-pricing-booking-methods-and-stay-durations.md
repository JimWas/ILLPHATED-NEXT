✅ Booking & Stay Tips
Choose your access type:

Pay-per-use (Miracle, Coral, etc.), airline-class privileges, or via Priority Pass/Credit Card programs.

Book ahead:

Miracle: book via GetYourGuide, WAUG, or voucher platforms.

Coral: must reserve ≥12 hours in advance online.

Airline lounges: use respective airline or third-party portals.

Allowed time: Most lounges cap at 2–3 hours; extra time may incur charges per hour.

Walk-ins: Miracle and some airline lounges are amenable to walk-ins—come early to ensure space.

Facilities vary: showers, food quality, Wi‑Fi reliability, massage zones differ—Miracle consistently ranks high.

🎯 Final Recommendation
Best for value & comfort: Miracle Lounge — high-quality food, reliable showers, spacious seating, and 24/7 operation.

Smaller investment: Coral Executive offers Thai-themed options and a pay-per-use convenience.

If you're on airline class: Use respective airline lounges (Thai, Turkish, Oman Air, Bangkok Airways).
