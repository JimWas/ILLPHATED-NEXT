14. “You looked like you were about to say something interesting, so I came over to listen.”

15. “Be honest—how often do you get approached by someone this charming?”

16. “You don’t seem like the ‘talk to random strangers’ type, but I’m betting I’m the exception.”

17. “You strike me as someone who knows exactly what they want. Am I right or dead wrong?”

18. “If this were a movie, this would be the part where we exchange witty lines and start something ridiculous.”
