is there food and drink for purchase at Rajadamnern Stadium

Yes, food and drinks are available for purchase at Rajadamnern Stadium. Inside the venue, you can buy snacks like chips, popcorn, and hot dogs, as well as drinks including water (around 30 THB, $1), soda, beer (around 100-200 THB, ~$3.50-$6 per cup or pint), and other alcoholic beverages. Prices for food items typically range from 80 to 150 THB ($1.50-$5). There’s also a premium menu in the VIP section with additional options, and some reviews mention free-flow beer and snacks with VIP tickets. Outside the stadium, to the front right, there’s an outdoor food court offering Thai dishes like fried rice and noodle soups for about 60 THB ($2) per plate, and fresh fruits for around 30 THB ($1) per bag. Note that outside food and drinks are not allowed inside the stadium due to a light security check at the entrance.

Do I have to go through security?

Yes, at Rajadamnern Stadium, there is a light security check at the entrance. Attendees typically go through a quick inspection where staff may check bags to ensure no outside food, drinks, or prohibited items are brought in. The process is generally straightforward and not overly invasive, similar to standard event venue security.

Can you smoke inside?

No, smoking is not allowed inside Rajadamnern Stadium. Thailand has strict regulations on smoking in public indoor spaces, and Rajadamnern Stadium, being a major venue, enforces these rules. You may be able to smoke in designated outdoor areas near the stadium, but it’s best to check with staff on-site for specific smoking zones.

can i bring in a water bottle?

No, you cannot bring in a water bottle to Rajadamnern Stadium. The venue has a light security check at the entrance, and outside food and drinks, including water bottles, are prohibited. You can purchase water inside the stadium for around 30 THB (~$1).
