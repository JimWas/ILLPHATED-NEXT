Where to Buy an Undead Mount at Level 40 in Turtle WoW – Complete Guide for Forsaken Players
If you’ve hit level 40 in Turtle WoW and saved up enough gold, it’s time to reward yourself with that sweet, skeletal ride every Forsaken dreams of. But where exactly do you get your undead mount, how much gold do you need, and what should you expect?

This guide breaks it all down so you can ride off into the dark horizon—Tirisfal-style.

🐎 How Much Gold Do You Need for Your Level 40 Undead Mount?
To purchase your Apprentice Undead Mount (60% speed), you’ll need:

Riding Skill (Apprentice): 20 gold

Undead Skeletal Horse: 9 gold

Total Cost: Around 29 gold (before reputation discounts)

💡 Pro Tip: If you are Honored or Revered with Undercity, you’ll get a discount, lowering the cost by up to 10–20%.

🗺️ Where to Buy Your Undead Mount in Turtle WoW
You can buy your undead mount in Brill, just outside of Undercity, in Tirisfal Glades.

📍 NPC: Zachariah Post – Undead Riding Trainer & Mount Vendor
Location: Brill, Tirisfal Glades

Coordinates: 58, 52

Trainer: Teaches Undead Horsemanship (Apprentice Riding)

Vendor: Sells Skeletal Horses

🧟‍♂️ Types of Skeletal Horses You Can Buy at Level 40
Brown Skeletal Horse

Blue Skeletal Horse

Red Skeletal Horse

Green Skeletal Warhorse (requires additional requirements, often level 60 or PvP)

Each comes with its own spooky flair, but all move at 60% base riding speed.

💰 Tips for Saving Gold Before Level 40
Avoid unnecessary purchases while leveling

Sell green/blue items on the Auction House

Use First Aid and bandages instead of potions

Pick up a gathering profession like Skinning or Herbalism

Do your class quest turn-ins for coin

🕷️ Bonus: Can Other Races Ride Undead Mounts?
Yes—non-Undead Horde players (like Orcs or Trolls) can ride Skeletal Horses, but only after reaching Exalted reputation with Undercity.

So if you’ve always wanted to see a Tauren on a skeletal steed, it’s technically possible. Just... a bit grindy.
