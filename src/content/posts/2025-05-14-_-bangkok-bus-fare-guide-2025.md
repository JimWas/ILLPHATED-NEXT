✅ Tips for Bus Travelers in Bangkok
Carry Small Change: While some buses accept cards, many still require cash payments.

Know Your Route: Use apps or online maps to determine bus numbers and stops.

Signal to Stop: Press the buzzer before your desired stop to alert the driver.

Be Patient: Traffic can be unpredictable; allocate extra time for your journey.
