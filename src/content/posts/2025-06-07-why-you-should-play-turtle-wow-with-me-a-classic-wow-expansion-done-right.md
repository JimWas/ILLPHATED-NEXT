🧩 What Turtle WoW Adds to Classic
1. A New Race: High Elves & Goblins (Playable!)
That’s right. Turtle WoW introduces High Elves for the Alliance and Goblins for the Horde as fully playable races, complete with custom starting zones, racial abilities, and immersive lore. It feels like content Blizzard should’ve made — but never did.

2. New Classes & Subclasses
You can play Druids of the Sea, Necromancers, and more. Every class has expanded talents and spells — balanced but refreshed. This isn’t retail garbage. It’s Classic+, but built by fans who respect the original feel.

3. Revamped Zones & Hidden Lore
Dead zones like Azshara and Desolace? Now alive with new quest lines, factions, and events. Turtle WoW breathes life into forgotten lands, adding dozens of custom quests and restoring cut lore that was removed from the original game.

4. Hardcore & Slow & Immersive Gameplay
Turtle WoW encourages slower, smarter leveling. Think: hardcore (no dying), roleplaying-friendly systems, and no rush meta. You can even walk to your quests and enjoy the scenery — and still feel rewarded.

5. Custom Mounts, Armor, and Events
From rare turtle mounts 🐢 to custom PvP tournaments, Turtle WoW surprises you at every corner. New models, custom gear, and seasonal events that actually matter.
