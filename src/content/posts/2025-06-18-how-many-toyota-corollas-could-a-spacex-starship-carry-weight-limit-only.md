🚀 How Many Toyota Corollas Could Fit in a SpaceX Starship? (By Weight)
SpaceX's Starship isn’t just the future of space travel—it’s also the future of heavy lifting. While most people picture satellites or astronauts on board, we asked a slightly more absurd question:

How many Toyota Corollas could Starship carry, based on weight alone?

Let’s get into the ridiculous (but very real) math.

📦 Starship Payload Capacity (To Low Earth Orbit)
According to SpaceX:

Starship’s payload capacity to LEO (Low Earth Orbit):

150,000 kg (330,000 lbs) in reusable mode

250,000 kg (550,000 lbs) in expendable mode

For this post, we’ll go with the reusable configuration, since that’s the future Elon wants.

🚗 Toyota Corolla Curb Weight
The average Toyota Corolla sedan weighs:

~3,000 lbs (1,360 kg)

This is curb weight—no passengers, no cargo. Just the car with fluids and gas, ready to drive off a spaceship.
